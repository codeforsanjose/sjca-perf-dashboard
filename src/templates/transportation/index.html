Transportation Department

In 2014-15, the Transportation Department’s (DOT) operating expenditures totaled $79 million,* about 26 percent more than ten years ago. DOT had 426 authorized positions, but staffing was still 10 percent lower than 10 years ago.

* DOT was also responsible for approximately $5.8 million of Citywide expenses in 2013-14, including about $2.1 million in parking citations/jail courthouse fees, and $1.3 million for sidewalk repairs. DOT also had authority over approximately $178 million in special funding and capital improvement programs for parking and traffic.
